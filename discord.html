RULES

Be Respectful:
Treat all members with kindness and courtesy. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks allowed.
Stay on Topic:
Keep conversations relevant and meaningful. Avoid spamming or flooding the chat.
Use Appropriate Channels:
Find the right channel for your discussions. Take a look at the channel descriptions to post in the right place.
Maintain a Safe Environment:
Explicit, NSFW, or offensive content is strictly prohibited. This includes text, images, links, and discussions.
No Advertising:
Obtain permission from staff before promoting external servers, products, or services.
Preserve Positivity:
Avoid excessive trolling, raiding, or disruptive behavior. Let's create a warm and inclusive atmosphere.
Respect Staff Decisions:
Trust our dedicated staff team and reach out to a moderator or administrator if you have concerns.
Constructive Discussions:
Keep debates civil and constructive. Disagreements are fine, but personal attacks are not tolerated.
Language Usage:
English is the primary language for general communication. Contact staff if you need assistance in another language.
Discord Guidelines:
Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
