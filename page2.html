1.No griefing

Griefing refers to intentionally destroying or damaging other players' builds or stealing their items. It disrupts the gameplay experience for others and is generally considered unacceptable.

2.No cheating or hacking

Players should not use any mods, hacks, or cheats that give them an unfair advantage over others. This includes x-ray texture packs, fly hacks, or any form of unauthorized modifications to the game. 

3.Respect others

Encourage players to treat each other with respect and refrain from engaging in any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive language. Bullying, racism, sexism, or any form of hate speech should not be tolerated.

4.No spamming or excessive advertising

Players should refrain from spamming the chat or promoting other servers excessively. It disrupts the server's communication and may annoy other players.

5.Build responsibly

Encourage players to build responsibly and considerate of the server's resources and aesthetics. Large-scale projects should be approved by server staff to ensure they align with the overall vision and design of the server. 

6.No unauthorized PvP

Define rules for player-versus-player (PvP) combat, specifying areas where PvP is allowed and where it is not. Ensure players obtain consent from others before engaging in PvP to prevent unwanted conflicts.

7.No griefing the environment

Players should avoid excessively deforesting or altering the landscape without a valid reason. Encourage them to replant trees and maintain the natural beauty of the server's environment.

8.Respect spawn areas

Spawn areas are often communal gathering points and should be respected. Encourage players not to build or disrupt the spawn area with unnecessary structures or actions.

9.No AFK farming or excessive automation

Discourage players from using automated systems that can cause lag or overload the server, such as large-scale AFK farms or redstone contraptions that constantly run.

10.Reporting and conflict resolution

Establish clear guidelines for reporting rule violations and resolving conflicts between players. Encourage players to report any issues they encounter to the server staff, who can handle them appropriately.
